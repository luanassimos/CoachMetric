The Problem

Performance issues stay hidden until they hit the studio.

Most operators find coaching issues after a member complaint, a missed evaluation, or a manager escalation. By then, the cost already shows up in experience, retention, or team confidence.

  • × Evaluation data lives in spreadsheets, notebooks, or nowhere at all.
  • × No consistent standard across studios means different managers make different calls.
  • × Regional leaders can't see who's declining until the trend is already six weeks old.
  • × Development plans exist on paper but rarely connect to evaluation outcomes.
30 days

A delayed review cycle is often enough for a manageable coaching issue to become a retention or member-experience problem.

2 cycles

is enough time for a weak pattern to hide in plain sight when evaluations are inconsistent across locations.

CoachMetric gives operators one place to standardize evaluations, watch trend changes, and close coverage gaps before they become broader studio problems.

Product

One system for evaluations, risk, and follow-up.

CoachMetric keeps coach evaluations, coverage tracking, and follow-up in one operating system so managers can act from a single source of truth.

Action Center

See the next operational move, and why it matters.

The Action Center turns score, trend, and evaluation coverage into a clear queue so managers know where to intervene first.

  • Priority index based on absolute score, trend direction, and coverage risk
  • Separate queues for critical, studio-level, and coach follow-up work
  • Direct links to inspect the studio, review coaches, or start an evaluation
  • Items stay open until the underlying signal resolves

How It Works

Fit it into the operating cadence quickly.

CoachMetric fits the weekly rhythm studio teams already use. No long reporting project. No extra admin layer to keep alive.

01
🏢

Set up your studios and coaches

Add your studio locations and coach roster. Assign roles, managers, and evaluation owners. Takes under an hour for most operations.

02
📋

Run structured evaluations

Use built-in evaluation templates or customize scoring areas for your brand. Each evaluation feeds directly into the coach's performance record and trend line.

03
📊

Monitor scores and risk in real time

The dashboard surfaces declining trends, coverage gaps, and at-risk coaches the moment they cross a threshold, with no manual report required.

04
⚡

Act from the Action Center

Every signal generates a prioritized action. Open a studio, start a development plan, or schedule a follow-up evaluation from a single queue.

Capabilities

Built for day-to-day studio operations.

📈

Score History & Trend Analysis

Every coach has a full evaluation history with a visual score trend. See best, worst, and average scores at a glance and identify whether improvement is real or a one-cycle spike.

→ Spot real improvement vs. one-cycle variance
🎯

Development Plans

Attach structured development plans directly to evaluation outcomes. Plans are tracked, dated, and reviewed as part of the next evaluation cycle instead of being stored in a separate document.

→ Development that actually connects to performance
🏆

Regional Benchmarking

Compare any studio against the regional average. See where each location ranks and which studios are dragging down the region without pulling a manual report.

→ Know which studios need regional-level attention
⏰

Evaluation Cadence Tracking

CoachMetric tracks evaluation schedules and flags overdue cycles before they become coverage gaps. Overdue, Due Soon, and On Track statuses are visible per studio and per coach.

→ No more missed reviews or invisible gaps
🔔

Risk Classification

Coaches are automatically classified as Strong, Acceptable, Below Standard, At Risk, or Critical based on score and trend together, not score alone. A declining 85% is not treated as healthy.

→ Risk that reflects reality, not just a number
🏢

Multi-Studio Access Control

Studio managers see their location. Regional managers see everything. Permissions and scope are set per user so each role gets the visibility they need and nothing they do not.

→ Right data, right person, right time
